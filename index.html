What We Do

Deals that work when a cash offer doesn't.

Most buyers can only say one thing: a lower price, in cash. We structure offers on terms — seller financing and other creative structures — so the seller can get their number and we can make the deal make sense. That opens up properties other buyers walk away from: long days on market, no equity, unique situations.

🤝 Seller-friendly terms

Offers built around the seller's real goal — their price, their timeline, monthly income if that serves them better than a lump sum.

📊 Real underwriting

Every property runs through our in-house underwriting engine before we make an offer. If we say a number, the math behind it is done.

🏡 Residential & beyond

Single-family, multi-family, and the odd creative opportunity. On-market or off. If the numbers work, we're interested.

The Deal Hunter Program

Find deals. We do the rest. You get paid.

Our deal hunters ("birddogs") are everyday people who spot properties and send them our way. You don't need a license, experience, or money — just hustle. We handle the underwriting, the offers, the negotiating, and the closing.

  • You get your own underwriting sheet — type in an address, our engine runs the numbers for you.
  • Weekly team calls and live support while you learn.
  • Real training on how we talk to agents and sellers.
  • You get paid when deals you bring in close.

Agents & Sellers

A buyer worth calling on the listing nobody's moving.

Got a listing sitting past 70 days? A seller who needs their number, not another lowball? That's exactly the call we want. We move fast on the analysis, we're honest about what we can and can't do, and agents stay protected on every deal we structure.

Agents: bring us the stale listing. We'll tell you quickly whether we're a fit, and if we make an offer, your commission is part of the structure — always.

Sellers: if you need your price, your payoff handled, or just to be done — tell us the story on the place. Terms can often get you what a cash buyer never will.
